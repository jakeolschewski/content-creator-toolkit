## Hook Formulas That Stop the Scroll

The first 1-3 seconds determine whether someone watches or scrolls past. Here are proven hook structures you can adapt to any niche:

### The Curiosity Gap
> "Nobody talks about [surprising thing] and it's costing you [desirable outcome]."

**Examples:**
- "Nobody talks about the YouTube algorithm change last week and it's costing you thousands of views."
- "No one mentions this free tool and it's why your Reels get 200 views."

### The Contrarian Take
> "Stop doing [common advice]. Here's what actually works."

**Examples:**
- "Stop posting at 'optimal times.' Here's what actually drives reach."
- "Stop using trending audio. The algorithm changed."

### The Proof Hook
> "I went from [bad state] to [good state] in [timeframe]. Here's exactly how."

**Examples:**
- "I went from 200 to 50K followers in 90 days. Here's the exact playbook."
- "I went from $0 to $4K/month with a 2,000-follower account. Here's what I did differently."

### The "What If" Hook
> "What if [common frustration] was actually [reframe]?"

### The List Tease
> "3 things I wish I knew before [milestone]. Number 2 changed everything."

## Content Templates

### The "Mini Documentary" (YouTube / Long-form TikTok)

```
1. Cold open (hook - 3 sec)
2. Context (why this matters - 15 sec)
3. Story/journey (the meat - 60-180 sec)
4. The turning point (what changed)
5. Lesson/takeaway
6. CTA (what to do next)
```

### The "Quick Win" (Reels / TikTok / Shorts)

```
1. Hook: "Here's a [topic] hack most people don't know"
2. Setup: One sentence of context
3. The tip: Show, don't tell. Screen record or demonstrate.
4. Result: Show the before/after or outcome
5. CTA: "Follow for more" or "Save this for later"
```

### The "Listicle Carousel" (Instagram / LinkedIn)

```
Slide 1: Bold claim or question (hook)
Slide 2-6: One tip per slide with brief explanation
Slide 7: Summary or bonus tip
Slide 8: CTA + "Share this with someone who needs it"
```

### The "Story-Lesson" (Any Platform)

```
1. "Last week something happened that changed how I think about [topic]..."
2. Tell the story (keep it tight, 3-5 sentences)
3. Extract the lesson
4. Make it actionable: "Here's what you can do today..."
```

### The "Myth Buster"

```
1. "Everyone says [common belief]. They're wrong."
2. Explain why the common belief exists
3. Show the evidence/data
4. Give the real answer
5. Actionable next step
```

## Script Frameworks

### The AIDA Framework (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action)

Best for: Sales-oriented content, product reviews, "day in my life" with a CTA

```
ATTENTION: Open with something unexpected or visually striking
INTEREST: "Here's why this matters to you specifically..."
DESIRE: Paint the picture of the outcome. Show results.
ACTION: Clear, single CTA. Don't give 5 options.
```

### The PAS Framework (Problem, Agitate, Solve)

Best for: Educational content, tutorials, "how I fixed X"

```
PROBLEM: Name the pain point your audience feels
AGITATE: Make them feel it. "You've tried X, Y, Z and nothing works..."
SOLVE: Present your solution. Walk them through it step by step.
```

### The "3-Act" Short-Form Script

```
ACT 1 (0-3 sec): Hook — pattern interrupt, bold statement, or question
ACT 2 (3-30 sec): Value delivery — the actual tip, story, or information
ACT 3 (last 3-5 sec): Payoff — the result, punchline, or CTA
```

**Pro tip:** Write your hook last. Create the content first, then figure out the most compelling way to open it.

## AI Tools for Creators

AI won't replace creators, but creators who use AI will outperform those who don't. Here's how to use it without losing your voice:

### Ideation & Research
- **ChatGPT / Claude** — Brainstorm content angles. Prompt: "Give me 20 content ideas about [topic] that would work as 60-second TikToks for [target audience]."
- **Google Trends** — Validate that people actually search for your topic before creating content about it.
- **AnswerThePublic** — Find the exact questions your audience is asking.

### Writing & Scripting
- Use AI to generate first drafts, then rewrite in your voice. Never post AI output directly — audiences can tell.
- **Prompt template:** "Write a 60-second video script about [topic]. Tone: [casual/professional/funny]. Target audience: [who]. Include a hook in the first line and a CTA at the end."

### Editing & Production
- **CapCut** — Free editing with AI-powered captions, effects, and templates.
- **Descript** — Edit video by editing text. Remove filler words automatically.
- **Canva** — Thumbnails, carousels, and graphics with AI-assisted design.

### Repurposing
- One long-form video can become 5-10 short clips, a blog post, a newsletter, and a carousel.
- **Prompt template:** "Take this transcript and create: 1) Three tweet-length takeaways, 2) An Instagram carousel outline (8 slides), 3) A LinkedIn post."

## Posting Schedule Guide

### TikTok (2026 Algorithm)
| Day | Posts | Best Times (ET) |
|-----|-------|-----------------|
| Mon-Fri | 1-3 | 7-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 7-9 PM |
| Sat-Sun | 1-2 | 10 AM-12 PM, 7-9 PM |

**Key insight:** Consistency matters more than timing. Post at the same times daily so the algorithm knows when to push your content.

### YouTube
- **Long-form:** 1-2x per week, same day/time
- **Shorts:** 3-5x per week
- **Best days:** Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday
- **Upload 2-3 hours before** your audience is most active (check YouTube Studio analytics)

### Instagram
- **Reels:** 4-7x per week (this is where reach comes from in 2026)
- **Carousels:** 2-3x per week (best for saves and shares)
- **Stories:** Daily (keeps you top of followers' feeds)
- **Static posts:** 1-2x per week max (lowest reach but good for brand)

### LinkedIn
- **Posts:** 3-5x per week
- **Best times:** Tue-Thu, 8-10 AM ET
- **Carousels and documents** outperform text-only posts by 2-3x

### The Real Secret
Track your own analytics for 30 days. Your audience's behavior matters more than any generic "best time to post" guide. Every niche is different.

## Monetization Strategies

### Tier 1: Low Effort, Low Revenue
- **Platform monetization** (TikTok Creator Fund, YouTube AdSense, Instagram bonuses)
- **Affiliate links** (Amazon Associates, ShareASale, individual brand programs)
- Realistic: $100-$1,000/month at 10K-50K followers

### Tier 2: Medium Effort, Medium Revenue
- **Sponsored content** (brand deals, UGC)
- **Digital products** (templates, presets, guides, ebooks)
- **Coaching / consulting** (1:1 or group)
- Realistic: $1,000-$10,000/month at 10K-100K followers

### Tier 3: High Effort, High Revenue
- **Courses and memberships**
- **Software / SaaS**
- **Agency services**
- **Licensing and syndication**
- Realistic: $10,000+/month at any follower count (revenue scales with product, not followers)

### The Math That Matters
You don't need millions of followers. You need:
- **1,000 true fans** who will each pay you $100/year = $100K/year
- Or **100 clients** at $1,000 each = $100K/year
- Or **10 premium clients** at $10,000 each = $100K/year

Focus on building an audience of buyers, not just viewers.

## Analytics & Optimization

### Metrics That Actually Matter

**Stop obsessing over:**
- Follower count
- Like count
- Total views

**Start tracking:**
- **Watch time / retention rate** — Are people actually consuming your content?
- **Save rate** — High saves = high value content (Instagram and TikTok both reward this)
- **Share rate** — Shares are the #1 signal for viral distribution
- **Click-through rate** — Are your thumbnails and hooks working?
- **Conversion rate** — Of the people who see your CTA, how many take action?
- **Revenue per 1,000 views (RPM)** — The only metric that pays your rent

### The 80/20 of Content Optimization
1. **Test hooks relentlessly.** Same content with a different hook can 10x your views.
2. **Study your top 10% of posts.** What do they have in common? Make more of that.
3. **Kill your bottom 20%.** What content types consistently underperform? Stop making them.
4. **Check retention graphs** (YouTube Studio, TikTok Analytics). Where do people drop off? Fix that moment.
5. **A/B test thumbnails** on YouTube. A better thumbnail on an existing video can double its CTR overnight.
